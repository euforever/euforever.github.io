Getting There

  • We recommend flying into Las Vegas Airport.
    • The time zone in Las Vegas is Pacific Daylight Time (3 hours behind New York).
    • It's about a 2.5 hour drive from Las Vegas to Springdale.
    • The time zone in Springdale is Mountain Daylight Time (2 hours behind New York; 1 hour ahead of Las Vegas).
  • You can also fly into Salt Lake City Airport.
    • The time zone in Salt Lake City (and Springdale) is Mountain Daylight Time (2 hours behind New York).
    • It's about a 5 hour drive from Salt Lake City to Springdale.
  • Another choice is St. George Regional Airport.
    • The time zone is also Mountain Daylight Time (2 hours behind New York).
    • It's about a 1 hour drive from St. George to Springdale.
  • There are easy car rentals at all airports and the drives are beautiful.
