Getting There

  • We recommend flying into Las Vegas Airport.
    • The timezone in Las Vegas is Pacific Standard Time (3 hours behind New York).
    • It's about a 2.5 hour drive from Las Vegas to Springdale.
  • You can also fly into Salt Lake City Airport.
    • The timezone in Salt Lake City (and Springdale) is Mountain Time Zone (2 hours behind New York).
    • It's about a 5 hour drive from Salt Lake City to Springdale.
  • There are easy car rentals at both airports and the drives are beautiful.
